Yixuan Wang 汪怡暄

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Electrical and Computer Engineering Department. Before entering UIUC. I was an undergraduate student in the University of Michigan, major in Computer Science. I also obtained a B.S.E. in Mechanical Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am working on the robotic manipulation under the supervision of Dr. Yunzhu Li and Prof. Katie Driggs-Campbell. I worked on deformable object tracking under the supervision of Dr. Dale McConachie in the Autonomous Robotic Manipulation Lab, investigated by Dr. Dmitry Berenson. I also worked on soft robot controlling based on perception information under the supervision of Dr. Audrey Sedal in the Compliant Systems Design Laboratory, investigated by Dr. Sridhar Kota.

I am activately looking for an internship position for 2024 summer. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research.
